About MyBOOK

MyBOOK is a platform designed to connect students who want to share and exchange academic books. We believe in making education more accessible and affordable through the power of community sharing.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where knowledge can be passed from one student to another, reducing the financial burden of purchasing new textbooks while promoting environmental sustainability.
